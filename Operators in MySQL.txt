Operators in MySQL:
====================

Operators are used to retrieve the records from the table as per the given condition.Operators are also used to perform the calculations over the tables.Operators are classified as follows:
1.Arthimetic operator ----> +,-, *, / ,//,%
   a)Addition(+)
   b)Substraction(-)
   c)Multiplication(*)
   d)Division(/)     ---> Float
   e)Floor Division(//) ---> Integer
   f)Modulus(%)   ---> another name remainder
2.Relational operators
    a)Equality operator
        i) =         ii) not column =
    b)Comparision operator
        i)<     ii) >    iii)<=    iv) >=
3.IN
4.NOT 
5.BETWEEN
6.LIKE
7.and
8.or
